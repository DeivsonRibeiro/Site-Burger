## 2024-01-08 - The Power of Image Optimization

**Learning:** Even without a complex build process, significant performance gains can be achieved by focusing on fundamental web performance principles. A single, unoptimized image was responsible for a 7.8MB payload on the initial page load.

**Action:** Always check for large, unoptimized images, especially those loaded above the fold. Converting to modern formats like WebP and compressing JPEGs is a high-impact, low-effort optimization that should be a primary target.
